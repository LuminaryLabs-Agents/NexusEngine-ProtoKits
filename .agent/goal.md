# Goal

Status: active

## Purpose

Extract reusable domains from read-only project repositories, preserve their useful feature union during merging, and incubate bounded NexusEngine ProtoKits with explicit subdomain proposals and promotion evidence.

## Success Criteria

- Source repositories remain unmodified.
- Every extracted capability has a reusable domain owner and natural subdomain map.
- Existing owners are reused before new kits are created.
- Merges preserve the union of useful behavior and source lineage.
- New proposals are explicitly experimental and remain inside declared domain boundaries.
- Runtime behavior has targeted headless, reset, snapshot, determinism, boundary, and performance evidence before promotion.
- Every pushed change is paired with a LuminaryLabs audit record.
